Customer Support Policy Manual

1. Introduction
Welcome to our customer support policy guide. This document outlines our customer support procedures, business hours, and escalation processes.

2. Business Hours
Our customer support team is available during the following hours:

Monday to Friday: 9 AM to 6 PM IST
Saturday: 10 AM to 2 PM IST
Sunday: Closed

Please note that we are closed on all major holidays. For urgent matters outside of business hours, please use our emergency contact system.

3. Contact Methods
- Phone: 1-800-SUPPORT
- Email: support@company.com
- Live Chat: Available during business hours
- Emergency: 1-800-EMERGENCY (24/7 for critical issues only)

4. Response Times
- General inquiries: Within 4 hours during business hours
- Technical issues: Within 2 hours during business hours
- Urgent matters: Within 1 hour during business hours
- Emergency issues: Within 30 minutes (24/7)

5. Support Levels
Level 1: General customer service representatives
Level 2: Technical support specialists
Level 3: Senior technical specialists
Level 4: Management escalation

6. Escalation Matrix
If your query is unresolved:
1. Level 1: Support representative (initial contact)
2. Level 2: Technical specialist (if technical issue)
3. Level 3: Senior specialist (complex issues)
4. Level 4: Management (escalation required)

7. Service Standards
- Professional and courteous service
- Clear communication in simple terms
- Follow-up on all issues until resolution
- Documentation of all interactions
- Customer satisfaction surveys

8. Quality Assurance
All support interactions are monitored for quality and training purposes. We strive for 95% customer satisfaction and resolve 90% of issues on first contact.
